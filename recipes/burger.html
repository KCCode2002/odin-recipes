Make the perfect burger

Why is this the perfect burger?

We have everything a burger could ever want here. Caramelized Onions. Homemade sauce. Brioche buns. Good Cheese. All within a short 15 minutes. Try it today!

What you'll need

  • Ground beef
  • Brioche buns
  • Your cheese of choice
  • Tomato slices
  • Onions
  • Lettuce
  • Mayonaise
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Butter

How to make the burger

  1. Shape your ground beef into patties
  2. Heat up a pan with a small amount of butter
  3. Get patties in the hot pan, season accordingly
  4. Slice onions to size, get them in the butter of the pan
  5. Slice some lettuce to size, rinse afterwards
  6. Combine 2 parts mayo, 1 part ketchup, and a touch of mustard into a container and mix!
  7. Get your buns in the toaster
  8. Flip burgers and onions
  9. Once the burger is cooked through, cheese and lid on, heat off
  10. Build your burger however you want, and enjoy!
